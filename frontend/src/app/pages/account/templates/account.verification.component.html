Account Verification

In order to be a LWF Forwarder, your account must be verified in 3 steps: ID verification, Address verification, Selfie ID.

If you use LWF as a customer/buyer only, you simply need the ID verification.

By following the verification procedure you agree to our terms and conditions.

Continue with the verification to become a Local Forwarder,
we just need a proof of residence and a picture with your ID.
