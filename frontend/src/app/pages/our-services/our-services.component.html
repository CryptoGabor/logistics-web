P2P Freight

Forwarding

Getting you closer to your favorite products.

If you can't get it, somebody can get it for you!

Have you ever experienced the pain of not being able to buy something because direct delivery to your home country isn’t available? Even in today's globalised world, such inconvenience continues to happen to thousands of people every day!

Unfortunately many companies do not ship all over the world because is not convenient for them, due to logistics costs or marketing strategies.

Is there a way to overcome these barriers? Yes! The LWF P2P shipment service.

Peer to peer​shipment means that normal people like you and me, that live in a country where your desired item is available, can receive it on behalf, repack it and send it to the buyer. All through a website, simple and fast!

Thanks to LWF you can access to a widespread network of trusted local forwarders. A sophisticated algorithm helps find the best shipment offer available, according to your parameters.

The strength of LWF P2P logistic systems relies on a decentralized and highly secure network, based on blockchain architecture. Beyond the algorithm that manages LWF, there are normal people that offer private shipment services, free from complex logistic protocols.

Every local forwarder must have been previously verified by LWF and compiles with the strict affiliation rules. In addition to that, all the shipments managed through LWF are covered by an international insurance policy that covers both the receiver and the forwarder.

LWF - Secure, Protected and Convenient:

  • ability to track your package on its journey - from collection until it’s signed by the receiver (including Proof of Delivery)
  • full insurance protection for the receiver (covering fraud, theft or loss of parcel)
  • you can pay in EUR, USD or cryptocurrencies (click for more info)

Instant

Delivery

Whatever you want, whenever you need it... LWF can deliver it quickly!​

LWF Instant Delivery is a fast on-demand logistic service. Thanks to a widespread network of LWF couriers, you can count on a quick and safe instant delivery service in your area.

The LWF platform connects customers with local couriers who can deliver anything, whether for you or for your business. Start a delivery request specifying your address, parcel specifications and desired delivery time. Any available courier within the network can accept your request and carry out the delivery in minutes.

Your item will travel in safe hands, since all the LWF local couriers are verified: when submitting your request, you will see the identity of the courier, the reviews and the mode of transport used for your delivery.

As with all purchases in the LWF network, payments for Instant Delivery can be issued in your preferred currency, including EUR, USD or cryptocurrencies.

How much time have you wasted in line at the post office to collect your parcels?

Forget about it! Now you decide when and where collect your package, freed from carriers or post office schedules.
