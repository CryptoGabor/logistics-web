OUR SERVICES

P2P Freight Forwarding

International shipments without borders.

If you can't get it, somebody can get it for you!

Unfortunately many companies do not ship all over the world. There a way to overcome these barriers: the LWF P2P shipment service. Peer to peer shipment means that normal people like you and me, that live in a country where your desired item is available, can receive it on your behalf, repack it and send it to you. All through a website, simple and fast!

Instant Delivery

Need something? Get it right now!

Whatever you need, LWF can deliver it quickly!

LWF Instant Delivery is a fast on-demand logistic service. LWF platform connects customers with local couriers who can deliver anything, whether for you or for your business. Start a delivery request specifying your address, parcel specifications and desired delivery time. Any available courier within the network can accept your request and carry out the delivery in minutes.

Package Collection

You decide when and where pick up your package.

Collect your parcel nearby, at your convenience

How much time have you wasted in line at the post office to collect your parcels? Through the LWF network of private pick-up points, you can save time by collecting your package by the nearest point and at your convenience. Before placing an order online, check the LWF portal for private collectors available in your area or nearby.
